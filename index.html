What I've Built

Featured Project · $1,000 Scholarship

Fragrance Engine

You know nothing about making a fragrance? Choose what you want it to smell like, adjust to your taste, and the engine will calculate a carefully refined and researched formula that is ready to be implemented at any reasonable scale. This requires a full-stack fragrance formulation system that converts scent goals into structured formulas using a deterministic scent-pyramid model. Features volatility and longevity modeling from physical properties, version-controlled formulas with diffs, IFRA compliance checking, and a custom olfactory taxonomy spanning 14+ scent families.

Designed and modeled a scaled-down process flow system in Onshape featuring a mixer, evaporator, and crystallizer with connecting piping networks. Built within a 20×16 ft concrete tabletop constraint with all units under the 3 ft height limit.
