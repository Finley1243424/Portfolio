About

Where Chemistry Meets Code

I'm a second-year Chemical Engineering student at Rochester Institute of Technology with a fascination for the space where scientific rigor meets creative problem-solving. My coursework spans thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and organic chemistry — but what excites me most is building tools that make engineering intuition computable.

That curiosity led me to develop a full-stack fragrance formulation engine — a project that earned a $1,000 endowed scholarship and combines physical chemistry modeling with software engineering. I approach every problem the same way: understand the fundamentals, prototype rapidly, and iterate until the solution is both elegant and functional.

I'm looking for a co-op where I can apply this blend of chemical engineering knowledge and computational thinking to real-world process challenges.
