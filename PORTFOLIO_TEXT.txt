3. PROJECTS SECTION

3a. Fragrance Engine (Featured)
Badge Text
Featured Project · $1,000 Scholarship
Title
Fragrance Engine
Description
You know nothing about making a fragrance? Choose what you want it to smell like, adjust to 
your taste, and the engine will calculate a carefully refined and researched formula that is ready 
to be implemented at any reasonable scale. This requires a full-stack fragrance formulation 
system that converts scent goals into structured formulas using a deterministic scent-pyramid 
model. Features volatility and longevity modeling from physical properties, version-controlled 
formulas with diffs, IFRA compliance checking, and a custom olfactory taxonomy spanning 14+ 
scent families.
